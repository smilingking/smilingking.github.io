The art of exaggeration

6 september, 2019

Hi everyone! So, I have been learning social skills for a long time and In this post, I am going to share a technique that I think is really helpful in making the conversation more fun and better.

I really like this one and I am sure you will love it and implement it in your life and have fun with it.

Remember, be creative and have fun, that's 90% of good social skills.

SO, a few months ago I was surfing on youtube and found a video of comedian Kenny Sebastian, where he was interacting with the audience and he was asking names and doing comedy using their names, I found it really interesting and I wanted to learn what he was doing.


I started researching and found something interesting and I am going to teach you about that. Comedy has a rule that there are jokes in almost all situations, It’s just what you say and how you say it that matters. 

Anything can be made funny using exaggeration, exaggeration is really easy and to make it easier, let me break it down for you. So, anything can be made funny using 3 steps.



ASSUME -> JUSTIFY -> EXAGGERATE


That's right, you can take literally anything like someone is wearing something strange today and assume something about it, justify it and make the exaggeration to make it funnier.


Example: 

Vidit: 
I am so exhausted from running.
Nilesh:
Who are you running from? Your dad? (assumption)
Looks like he caught you watching some nice videos (justification)
And don’t tell me it was one of Amanda Cerny (exaggeration)


--------------------------------------------------------------

That is a good example of how we can use exaggeration to make conversations funnier and engaging.
one more example:
--------------------------------------------------------------
Nilesh:
So, Where are you from?
Maya:
london
Nilesh:
London is expensive (assumption, but it is also a fact)
Like whenever I meet a dude from London, they keep complaining about their expenses (justification)
When I will go to London, I will go bankrupt at the same time (exaggeration)
------------------------------------------------------------

It is not necessary that you follow the same pattern all the time, mix it up, play with it and be creative with your words
