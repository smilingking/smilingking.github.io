Welcome to NT-social

23 september, 2019

“IN THIS TERRIFYING WORLD, ALL WE HAVE ARE THE CONNECTIONS THAT WE MAKE” This line from BOJACK HORSEMAN (Netflix) has to be reminded to everyone, most of the things we do are for people, we work hard to impress our parents, we crack jokes so that we can make people laugh, we wear good clothes to make a good impression on others, we share out happiness with others and others help us to get us out of depression or hard times. When we are surrounded by good people, people we share something with, those we love, the people we seek love from. Those are the ones that help us live this life happier and give us reason to be our best.



We can make this world happier and better by making more amazing connections, by meeting the kind of people we like and those who accept us for who we are. For that to happen, we have to start looking at others as a friend rather than someone we have to compete with for likes, we need to learn to forgive people and love them, we need to help each other to make this journey of life wholesome.
 

That is the reason NT-social is born, social skills and social life is something that I have struggled with for a long time, being shy and not talking with people even when I want to. Luckily I have been learning it for quite some time and I can say from experience, learning social skills and personal development has to be the best part of my life, It helped me become happier and helped my flaunt my flaws and I will keep doing it. I have decided to continue this journey with everyone and create a community where we help each other and become more loving and compassionate towards each other, where we help each other become more social and find the ones that we connect deeply.


From now on NT-social will post a lot of stuff related to social skills and personal development, so follow us on every platform. We are also going to release a youtube video by the end of this week.
